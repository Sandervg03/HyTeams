Welcome to HyTeams!

What is HyTeams?

HyTeams is a platform to create or find a team to play various hypixel skyblock teambased quests and events like Kuudra, Dungeons or the Mythological Rituals.

If you want to find some teammates to play with, HyTeams is the right place for you! Just make an account and start creating or joining a team.dwad

How do I get started with HyTeams?

Register an account at the Register page. After registering your account and logging in, you will be able to start looking for teams on the Teams page. or create your own team at the My Team page.

What quests and events does HyTeams support?

As of now, we only support Dungeons, more is on it's way.

Kuudra will be first on our agenda, if you want updates on the process, make sure to turn on update notifications at the Build Project page.
